Reflections

What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

So far, I'm excited to be part of such an in-depth learning experience. It already feels like the start of a journey as opposed to just the start of a course.

What are your impressions?

I love the idea of being in the kitchen and becoming a creator. It's already such a different way of thinking about learning.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I think I have a tendency to have a restaurant attitude so I want to actively engage with a kitchen attitude instead.

Have your expectations changed? If so, how?

Yes. I'm already seeing how this is more than just a cookie-cutter or "restaurant" course, which makes me really happy.

Are your excited to participate in this kinf og learning environment? Nervous?

I'm both! It's all new and it's the start of a completely different adventure for me, so I feel excited and nervous most of the time.
