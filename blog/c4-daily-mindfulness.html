What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

The process was very similar to social work! Social work has different terms for the techniques involved but the principles are the same. I learnt that I could benefit from applying those skills in everyday life. My sister said she felt listened to.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

I liked the idea of having a buddy, and I happen to have a flatmate who has just been on a mindfulness course so we decided to try it out together. I also liked the idea of doing less than you can - I've used a similar process for exercise before and have found it works well for me.
